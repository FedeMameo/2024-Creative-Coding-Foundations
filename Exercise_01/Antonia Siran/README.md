# **Honest Wedding Wishes**

## **Author**
Antonia Andreea Siran

## **Concept Description**

Honest Wedding Wishes is a fun and playful take on typical wedding messages. Instead of the usual “Best Wishes” or “Happily Ever After,” this project reveals the more honest, and sometimes sarcastic, thoughts behind those classic wedding phrases. Users can click on buttons to reveal humorous and ironic responses, adding a lighthearted twist to wedding wishes.

This project is perfect for anyone with a sense of humor who enjoys also a bit of sarcasm. :)
